Lasagna

Lasagna is a dish made out of Lasagne pasta and generally made
with parmesan cheese, white sauce, and a tomato sauce. It is a very simple yet delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Spices
  • Lasagne Pasta
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up oven to 375
  2. Cook all of the meat
  3. Boil a pot of water and cook the lasagne
    for 20minutes
  4. Assemble the stuff in the pan and put it in the oven
