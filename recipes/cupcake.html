Cupcake

A cupcake is a delicious bakery treat usually a soft muffin topped with some sort of cream.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder & Salt
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Frosting

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, eggs in a bowl
  3. Put batter in a muffin tin
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes
