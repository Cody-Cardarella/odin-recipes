Pumpkin Pie

Pumpkin pie is a common dish usually seen during fall
and on thanksgiving made out of...pumpkin!

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin puree
  • Condensed Milk
  • Eggs
  • Cinnamon, Ginger, Nutmeg, Salt
  • Pie Crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Whisk everything into a bowl
  3. Pour puree into the pie crust
  4. Bake for 35-40min
